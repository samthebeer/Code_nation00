Table Top Role Playing Games

There are several Table Top Role playing games (TTRPGs) that I run and played.

The first TTRPG I played was Dungeons and dragons (D&D) 3.5 but I haven't played that since 2014 when 5e was released, I am not yet familiar with the 2024 update because I prefer to play indy RPGs.

A role plaing game is a form of colabrative storytelling, where the players inhabit the charicters and decide what they do. The rules or mechanics are a method for deciding what happens when the players try to do things. There is often a Gamesmaster, or Dungeon master in D&D, who runs the game, enforces* the rules, and describes the world.

Not all TTRPGs

Dungeons and dragons is the most popular and well known tabletop roleplaing games.
