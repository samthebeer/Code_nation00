Molten Salt Electrolysis

Molten salt is useful for electrolysis due to it having a different potential window to water, this means that aluminium can be refined using the Hall–Héroult process.

This technology is mature, I was working on refining refactory metals using the FFC Cambridge process This relies on the ability to disolve elemental Calcium which acts as a reductant.
